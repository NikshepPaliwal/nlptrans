The Visvesvaraya Founder's Roadmap

How to transition your PhD research into a scalable startup entity.

1

Research & IP Creation

Focus your PhD dissertation on a localized Indian dataset. Publish papers, but prioritize filing patents through your university's incubation cell. Ensure your ML architecture is optimized for low-compute environments (Edge AI).

2

Prototyping & Grants

Utilize your fellowship stipend and apply for MeitY (Ministry of Electronics and IT) startup grants like TIDE 2.0. Build a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) and test it with a small cohort (e.g., local farmers, a single district hospital).

3

Spin-off & Scaling

Register via Startup India. Partner with NGOs or government bodies for data access and distribution. Transition from academic code to production-grade, scalable cloud infrastructure tailored for low-bandwidth users.
